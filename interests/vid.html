It all began in 2006 with a promise I made to a SPICMACAY volunteer: that I will make a video recapitulating all the events that SPICMACAY chapter organized. At that time, I only knew that there was an application called "windows movie maker" and have not really used it. I sat on the evening before the last program of the season, researched about SPICMACAY, finalized the script, narrated the script with voice-over, used a combination of the photographs and videos I have. I completed the video in about ~18hrs. It is called

    the year that was
It was just the begining of my adventures in all aspects of video production: editing, cinematography, acting, choreography, lyrics, production management etc... The only department I could not venture into is music. I do not how to play any instrument. Now, I have over 22 videos to my credit, which is ever expanding ...
